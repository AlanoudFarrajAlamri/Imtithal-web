# 📄 Imtithal (امتثال)

## Project Description
**Imtithal** is a web-based platform designed to simplify business compliance for entrepreneurs in Saudi Arabia. It helps users understand and navigate legal and regulatory requirements with ease, enabling them to start their businesses with confidence and clarity.

This project was developed as part of a hackathon participation using **VibeCoding**, focusing on building innovative, user-centered solutions efficiently and rapidly.

The platform provides a structured and user-friendly guide covering essential steps for establishing a business, with a strong focus on local regulations and compliance standards.

---

## 🚀 Key Features
- Simplified guidance for starting a business  
- Clear breakdown of legal and regulatory requirements  
- User-friendly interface tailored for beginners  
- Focus on Saudi market compliance  

---

## 🎯 Project Goal
To empower entrepreneurs—especially fresh graduates—to easily understand business setup requirements and reduce the complexity of regulatory procedures.
